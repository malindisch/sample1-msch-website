MALINDI sub-county hospital visitors policies

1. Duration of visit: Visiting hours are limited to certain times and the duration of the visit may also be restricted, depending on the condition of the patient.

2. Maximum number of visitors: Only two visitors allowed per patient at a time.

3. Age restriction: Only visitors aged 13 years and above are allowed into the wards and the hospital.

4. Identification: All visitors will be required to produce a valid identification document such as a national identity card or passport.

5. Cleanliness and Dress code: All visitors to the hospital are expected to be clean and appropriately dressed.

6. Patient privacy: Visitors are prohibited from taking photos or videos while in the hospital. They are also required to respect the privacy of other patients.

7. Restricted areas: Visitors are not permitted to enter restricted areas such as the High Dependency unit (HDU) or the operation theatre.

8. Covid-19 precautionary measures: All visitors will be required to wear a face mask and sanitize their hands before entering the hospital premises and the wards.

9. Children: No children are allowed into the wards unless there are special circumstances and with the permission of the hospital management.

10. Refusal of visitors: The hospital reserves the right to deny access to any visitor who does not comply with the visiting policies or who poses a risk to the safety and health of the patients, staff, or other visitors.

11. Visitor identification: All visitors must carry a valid visiting card and should register at the front desk before entering the ward.

12. Infection control measures: Visitors must follow the infection control measures like hand washing, wearing masks when required, and following the isolation precautions if a patient is in isolation.

13. Food and drink: Visitors are not allowed to bring food and drinks from outside the hospital for the patients.

14. Noise level: Visitors must maintain a quiet environment within the ward and should avoid making any noise that may disturb other patients.

15. Security: Visitors are advised not to bring valuables into the ward and are responsible for their belongings. Hospital management will not be responsible for any loss or theft of visitor property.

16. No smoking policy: The hospital is a smoke-free environment. Therefore, smoking is not allowed within the hospital premises.

17. Disruptive behavior: Any disruptive behavior by visitors towards patients, staff or other visitors will not be tolerated and may result in expulsion from the ward and possible legal action.

18. Feedback: The hospital welcomes feedback from visitors on how we can improve the services provided for the comfort and safety of the patient. Visitors can approach the hospital management with their feedback and suggestions.

Please help us keep the hospital safe and pleasant for everyone
