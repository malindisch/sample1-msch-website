who we are

Welcome to Malindi Sub-County Hospital, a public hospital located in the heart of Malindi that is committed to providing high-quality healthcare services to the people of Malindi and its surrounding areas. At Malindi Sub-County Hospital, we understand that access to quality healthcare is a basic human right. That's why we have invested heavily in modern facilities and state-of-the-art equipment to ensure that our patients receive the best possible care. We are staffed by highly trained and experienced medical professionals who are dedicated to providing personalized care for each patient.Our hospital offers a wide range of medical services, including emergency care, general surgery, obstetrics and gynecology, pediatrics, internal medicine, orthopedics, radiology, laboratory services, pharmacy services, and more. We also offer specialized clinics for chronic diseases such as diabetes and hypertension. We believe that every patient deserves respect and dignity regardless of their background or financial status. That's why we have made it our priority to provide affordable healthcare services without compromising on quality. As a public hospital, we are committed to serving the community through various outreach programs such as health education campaigns and free medical camps. We also collaborate with other stakeholders in the healthcare sector to improve access to healthcare services in our region. At Malindi Sub-County Hospital, we are passionate about improving the health outcomes of our patients. We strive to continuously improve our services through regular training programs for our staff and investing in new technologies that enhance patient care. Thank you for choosing Malindi Sub-County Hospital as your preferred healthcare provider. We look forward to serving you with excellence and compassion.

our mission

  • To provide high-quality and affordable healthcare services to the community through optimal use of resources and innovative healthcare technologies. We are committed to providing patient-centered care that is holistic and culturally responsive. Our goal is to improve the health outcomes of our patients by focusing on prevention and early detection of illnesses, and providing timely diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitative services

our vision

  • To be a leading healthcare provider in the region characterized by excellence, compassion, and innovation. We envision a future where our hospital is recognized for its commitment to providing patient-centered healthcare services that are accessible, affordable, and of the highest quality. We strive to be a center of excellence that attracts and retains the best healthcare professionals and encourages innovation and research. We aspire to be a community partner that is responsive to the needs of the residents, and provides a nurturing and healing environment for patients and their families.
